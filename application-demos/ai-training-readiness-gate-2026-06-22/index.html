AI Training Plan concept stub

Readiness before the plan changes.

A small feature stub for endurance athletes who need their plan to react to real life. The tool turns simple daily signals into an explainable recommendation before an AI coach adjusts the workout.

Explainable Shows why the recommendation changed, so athletes do not blindly follow model output.
Coach-ready Creates a short handoff note a human coach or support team could review.
Safe first Uses deterministic checks for risk signals before any AI-generated adjustment.

The production version would pull baselines from Garmin, Strava, Intervals.icu, or Apple Health. This stub keeps the logic visible so the product team can discuss thresholds, UX, and where an LLM should enter the workflow.
